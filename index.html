Active Rules

  • 1. Start with START, end with STOP
  • 2. At least one movement (F or B)
  • 3. PICK only on an object; DROP after PICK; no double PICK
  • 6. Movement costs energy (max 3); RECHARGE resets to full
  • 8. At least 2 complete PICK-DROP tasks (overwrites #4)
  • 10. No two turns in a row (overwrites #5)
  • 14. |left turns − right turns| ≤ 2 at all times
